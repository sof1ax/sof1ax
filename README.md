## About Me
Growing up, I was into everything — sports, art, languages, books — but the one constant was my computer. Even when I didn't have internet, I loved exploring, tinkering, and figuring out how things worked.

I studied Management Information Systems and have worked across IT support and data/analytical roles. Over time, I moved from backend to frontend, to full-stack, and even picked up design. Learning to code last year is what really changed everything — it opened a door where I could finally build the ideas I used to only imagine.

My curiosity used to feel like a weakness, but in tech, it became my advantage. Every new skill connects to the next, and every project teaches me something different.

If you’re someone who loves “too many things,” the truth is: you’re not lost — you’re built for tech.
